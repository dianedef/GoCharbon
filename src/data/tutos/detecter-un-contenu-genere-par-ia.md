thinks about all this. Because if a tool like this exists now, it stands to reason that Google could

Implement something similar in an upcoming update. But you've got to remember that with Google,

it always comes back to creating high-quality content. With them, it's not about if an AI

in their spam policies, which I'll link to in the description box for you to read at your leisure.

generated (or Auto-generated content) is content that's been generated programmatically

without producing anything original or adding sufficient value. Instead, it has been generated

for the primary purpose of manipulating search engines and not helping users." That's the key,

right? Without producing anything original, without adding sufficient value. I'll also draw

your attention to an article published and Wired back in April, which I'll also link to below,

a Page's ranking if used to genuinely help web Surfers. 'If the primary purpose of the
