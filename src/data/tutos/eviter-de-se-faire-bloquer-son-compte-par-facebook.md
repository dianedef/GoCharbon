    Hi Diane,Thank you for your interest in Scalify and for reaching out to the Scalify team.
    
    You can make every precaution and Facebook can still hold the right to disable your account without any explanation.
    
    However, here are essential steps you can take to prevent this:
    
    1. Create your Facebook profile with your authentic name and picture
    2. Facebook saves the IP address of banned accounts. When you create your new ad account try creating in a different address, this address should match your billing address on your credit card
    3. Make sure your Facebook page has a quality score above 2.0
    4. Facebook does not trust new ad accounts, for the first months, you want to launch ad campaigns with no more than 3 ad sets, then you can gradually scale up your campaigns and budgets over time.
    5. Before and after creating an ad creative or writing ad copy, make sure to run a fast check on the content for possible policy violations: [https://www.facebook.com/policies/ads/](__MASK_0__) click on learn more in each section to get examples.
