### WordPress Healthcheck

[WP Umbrella analyzes WordPress sites](https://wp-umbrella.com/features/health-checks-security-monitoring/?fpr=kasare), after which it will provide **suggestions for enhancing website** security, search engine optimization, and overall performance.

WP Umbrella **looks at all areas that can affect** a site’s security, no matter how big or small those aspects may be. WP Umbrella pays attention to factors such as any SEO and/or cache plugins in use, the site’s average ping rate, if alt texts are inserted, the ability for search engines to crawl the website, and if images are being compressed. No stone is left unturned.

**After evaluation**, you will be provided ways and resources to improve areas that may be lacking. WP Umbrella’s recommendations may help you improve user experiences and reduce site loading times.
