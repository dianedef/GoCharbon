# Rumble studio

Rumble Studio est passé par le site d'Anthony Nevo, [Vie De Dingue](http://viededingue1.com/F6JPnB)

Rumble Studio est une plateforme de création de podcast asynchrone (qui gère aussi les vidéos) interactive en ligne qui permet aux utilisateurs de créer des interviews pour leur podcast sans avoir à échanger face à face avec la personne.

### Pour qui ?

Rumble Studio est une application web destinée aux personnes qui souhaitent créer des podcasts et des vidéos interactifs sans avoir à se rencontrer en personne. Elle offre une plateforme en ligne qui permet aux utilisateurs de créer des interviews pour leur podcast sans avoir à se réunir physiquement.

Ce type de podcast et vidéos sont surtout destiné à un usage “pragmatique”, comme par exemple l’interview de candidats potentiels pour un nouveau poste, la récolte de témoignages clients, les podcast dans le domaine du business. Pour un podcast sur le développement personnel ou le contact humain et l’échange en temps réelest très important, il sera moins bien adapté.

### Intégrations

Les utilisateurs types de Rumble Studio sont des équipes de marketing, de communication, les solopreneurs qui veulent faire un podcast, mais aussi ceux qui créent du contenu et souhaitent récolter de l’information audio ou vidéo à grande échelle de leur audience

### **🤓** Comment l’utiliser :

1. Créez un compte sur Rumble Studio.
2. Définissez vos informations de profil et enregistrer vos questions
3. Invitez vos invités à enregistrer leurs réponses.
4. Écoutez et modifiez les réponses.
5. Publiez le contenu sur votre site Web ou partagez-le directement sur les plateformes de médias sociaux.

💡 The ideafor Rumble Studio began in 2018, when I launched the Voice Tech Podcast. After 100+ interviews with experts in voice technology, I had a great personal brand.

⌛ However, podcasting was very time-consuming, and releasing a weekly episode ate up my entire weekend. Planning, scheduling, recording, editing, publishing, promoting, monetising... I started to struggle to release episodes consistently (a.k.a. podfade). Worse, I couldn’t find any tools designed to speed up production.

🦾 Then I realized that the latest voice technologies, such as synthetic voices and conversational A.I., could automate much of my work as a podcaster.

🛠️ In September 2020, I started building Rumble Studio with my co-founder Joris Guerry, a world-class data scientist and SaaS developer.

🧑‍🚀 Our mission: to make podcasting 10X faster and easier for everyone.

⚡ We invented a new way to podcast: asynchronous interviews. Write some questions, send your guest(s) a link, and let Rumble run the interviews. This avoids scheduling live calls, and makes podcasting scalable, efficient and fun! Crowdsource audio from many guests at once to create multiple episodes fast, and generate huge engagement with your community.

🤖 You may ask, “What about the spontaneity of the conversation?”. Yes, multi-turn interactions are important. Our data scientists are building A.I. generated follow-up questions to extend the conversations. We’re adding synthetic voices for fast audio generation and corrections, question recommendations, podcast hosting & more. Check out our Roadmap!

🎧 That said, you can produce great async podcasts today, and enjoy time and cost savings of 90%. You just need good guests, good questions and good audio quality. Rumble Studio gives you all three!

Branding kits:

A branding kit is a set of visual and audio customisation settings used to personalize the interview for guests. You can add sets of colors, fonts, audio packs and more, to restrict the customization settings to those approved for a particular project or client. They are useful if you're creating interviews for different purposes (e.g. different podcasts), or for different clients (as an agency).

You can select a branding kit, use the elements to customise one or more interviews, then later you can edit the branding kit elements and use it again for a different interview(s). It’s just a set of options to save you time.

Video:

We are adding functionality for creators and guests to record video directly into Rumble Studio now. Video will be made available on all plans, to all users regardless of when they signed up. The exact details of this feature on each plan are yet to be confirmed i.e. the input/output video resolution, and hours of video recorded/exported per month etc.

We are primarily interested in helping creators produce audio content, and in the short/medium term, will only add video features to support this objective. For example, we do not currently have plans to add video editing or video merging to our Mix feature. But audio tracks from videos will be available in the audio Mix feature.
