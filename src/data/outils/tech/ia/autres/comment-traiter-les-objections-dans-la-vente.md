## Mastering Sales Objections: The Frisbee Technique Unveiled

Are you tired of stumbling over objections during your sales pitches? Do you wish there was a more graceful way to address customer concerns and objections? Look no further than the Frisbee Technique, a powerful method developed by sales expert Jean Pierre Girod to tackle objections head-on while maintaining rapport and respect with your potential clients.

### Understanding the Frisbee Technique

Derived from martial arts principles, the Frisbee Technique teaches sales professionals to harness the energy of objections and redirect them smoothly, much like catching and throwing a Frisbee. The core idea is to receive objections gracefully, understand them thoroughly, and respond softly to guide the conversation towards a positive resolution.

### Three Movements of Response

1. **Reception**: When faced with an objection, the first step is to receive it without resistance. Instead of viewing objections as barriers, see them as opportunities to engage with your prospect. By acknowledging their concerns without confrontation, you lay the groundwork for a productive dialogue.

2. **Handling**: Take a moment to understand the objection fully and prepare a thoughtful response. Avoid rushing to counter the objection; instead, focus on addressing the underlying needs of your prospect. By demonstrating patience and empathy, you can defuse tension and build trust.

3. **Return**: Softly redirect the objection back to the prospect, fostering collaboration and mutual understanding. Offer alternative perspectives or solutions that align with their needs and goals. By involving the prospect in the conversation, you empower them to take ownership of the decision-making process.

### Applying the Frisbee Technique

Let's consider an example: when a prospect says, "You're too expensive," respond with empathy and reassurance. Acknowledge their concerns about budget while emphasizing the value and long-term benefits of your solution. Offer options such as discussing added value or adjusting the offer to fit their budget, demonstrating flexibility and commitment to their satisfaction.

### Behavioral Tips for Success

- Maintain a serious yet relaxed demeanor during objections.
- Avoid rushing responses; take the time to understand and empathize with your prospect.
- Infuse your responses with warmth and empathy, showing genuine concern for their needs and concerns.

### Versatility Beyond Sales

While the Frisbee Technique is primarily designed for handling objections in sales, its principles are applicable in various contexts, including management, relationships, and personal interactions. By mastering the art of graceful objection handling, you can navigate challenging situations with confidence and professionalism.
