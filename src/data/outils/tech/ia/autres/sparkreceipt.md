We currently have an average rating of 4.87 tacos on AppSumo, which is 0.03 short of being eligible in the Customer Favorites category. We don't get that much help directly from AppSumo in form of big newsletters features etc - so if you'd like to help, please give your tacos to a great use! 🌮

And unlike other platforms, long detailed reviews do not matter so much here, but instead the single most important thing is just the total count of ratings. So if you're feeling lazy - just use the couple of seconds it takes to rate the app with stars. 👍
3. Download velocity - well, if you haven't downloaded the mobile app yet, please do. This will rank the app higher in both the conversion rate and the raw number of installs.
