Based on the search results, here are the key details about MagellanTV's pricing:

1. Monthly plan: $6.99 per month
2. Quarterly plan: $17.97 every three months (which works out to $5.99 per month)
3. Annual plan: $59.88 per year (which works out to $4.99 per month)
4. There's also a lifetime subscription option available for $159.97 through StackSocial, which is a significant discount from the regular $999 price.
5. MagellanTV offers a free 7-day trial for monthly memberships and a 14-day trial for annual memberships.
6. All plans include 4K streaming at no extra charge.
7. MagellanTV allows streaming on up to 5 devices simultaneously.
8. The service is ad-free on all plans.

Compared to other streaming services, MagellanTV is generally less expensive than mainstream options like Netflix or Hulu, but more expensive than some other documentary-focused services like CuriosityStream. The lifetime subscription offer through StackSocial appears to be a particularly good value if you're interested in long-term access to the service.
