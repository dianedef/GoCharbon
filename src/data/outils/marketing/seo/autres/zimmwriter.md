Hi. My name is Matt Zimmerman and I'm the creator of ZimmWriter. Let me share with you a little bit about it:

ZimmWriter is the world's first AI content writing software for Microsoft Windows. It lets you use the AI provided by OpenAI right on your desktop!

It works virtually anywhere you can edit text, such as in your browser, in Wordpress, Facebook, Frase, Surfer SEO, Google Docs, Gmail, Microsoft Word, Notepad, Visual Studio, etc.

Here are some of its unique features:

**#1 - Magic Command Writing**

- The bread and butter of this program. It allow you to use AI virtually anywhere in Windows that lets you enter text (e.g., MS Word, Gmail, Google Docs, Google Chrome, Firefox, a new post on Facebook, Wordpress, Notepad, etc). You can even scrape a URL and summarize it using a magic command.
    

**#2 - Continue Writing & Re-Writing**

- Just like the Magic Command writing above, but instead of writing a Magic Command, simply highlight something you already wrote, press the appropriate keyboard trigger, and AI will continue your thought or rewrite it.
    

**#3 - Magic Menu & Advanced Triggers**

- Store up to 16 magic commands and easily access them anywhere through the Magic Menu. You can also customize them with special options such as background information, literary devices, lists, voice, audience personality, etc.
    

**#4 - 1-Click Blog Post Generation**

**#5 - SEO Blog Writer**

- Includes website caching
    

**#6 - Bulk Blog Writer (Up to 1,000 Articles at Once)**

- Includes SERP scraping (requires a ScrapeOwl API)
    
- Includes website caching
    

**#7 - Bulk Webpage Rewriter (Up to 1,000 URLs at Once)**
