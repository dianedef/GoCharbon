Have you noticed how complex customer journeys have become over the last decade?

An average customer now interacts with brands across 20 channels before they come to a purchase décision.

This holiday season (and beyond), you must target customers across multiple touchpoints or risk losing out on brand awareness, engagement, and sales.

Take it from other marketers, who are now using 8 channels on average to engage with customers.

Rather than dumping all your ad budget into Meta or Google, consider spreading it across multiple channels including:

- Retail marketplaces (e.g. Walmart Connect)
- Growing social media platforms (e.g. TikTok, Snapchat)
- Niche search engines (e.g. Bing, Pinterest)
- Programmatic demand-side platforms (e.g. The Trade Desk)

But, multichannel marketing is just the beginning of an effective holiday advertising strategy.

You’ll still need to…

- _Define your target audience_
- _Plan your media mix_
- _Select the right KPIs_
- _Test your creatives_
- _Gather data from disparate sources and measure ad performance_

In our latest guide, we talk all about how to get through each step, including stratégies to collect 1st party data in an IDFA-less world.
