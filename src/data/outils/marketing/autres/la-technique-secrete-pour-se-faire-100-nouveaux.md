## En Ligne
### Hiki
[Neurodivergent Traits, Identities, and Conditions (Nonexhaustive list) - Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lQzu-de8vciH2Um5hTqkRGxlg4CcqNnQF-kPTvvQQtc/edit)
Five years ago, a small team of neurodivergent people set out to create an accessible online space where Autistic adults could meet new people, develop meaningful relationships, and find a sense of belonging in a world that does not always understand our differences. 

  
Since then, **our community has grown to nearly 200,000 users worldwide**, and we are thrilled to see the best friendships, partnerships, marriages, and even DnD parties that have all started on Hiki.  
  
Over the years, we've received hundreds of thousands of emails, DMs, and comments from non-Autistic people with ADHD, OCD, Down Syndrome, BPD, stutters, and more—all asking if they are welcome on Hiki. From day one, we’ve been proud to develop this platform by listening to and meeting the needs of our community, so we turned to you and asked: What makes for a _truly great_ social connection? Is it our _diagnoses and labels_ that matter most, or is it our _shared traits and experiences_ that bring us together?  
 

Through many conversations and feedback, one thing became clear: **regardless of the labels we use, all neurodivergent people want to feel safe, connected, and understood.** We surveyed our members, and 87% of our existing Autistic community said that they would love to welcome other ND adults onto the platform.  
 

### So today, we are thrilled to announce that **Hiki is opening its virtual doors to [all who identify as neurodivergent](https://hikiapp.us3.list-manage.com/track/click?u=ff7f9c639a624639f81042e4b&id=d644ebfd1e&e=437f2bdfbb)**, because we all deserve to be celebrated for our quirks and loved through our challenges; we all deserve relationships built on mutual understanding and accommodation; we all deserve a space to connect and flourish.  
 

Most modern dating and social apps encourage you to put your “best foot forward,” which sounds a lot like masking to us. We believe that **radical authenticity** is the fastest way to find your people, so we’ve crafted new profile customization options that center the support needs, communication styles, and unique traits that make for the most meaningful relationships.   
 

For the past five years, we have built Hiki with the support of volunteers, a few investors, and a small team of committed neurodivergent people. While most social platforms and dating apps profit from selling their user data to big advertising companies, Hiki has never, and will never, sell you out or fill your feed with pushy, overstimulating ads.  
 

### Finally, we are proud to announce the launch of **Hiki Premium,** which includes many of your most requested features:

- See everyone who has sent you a ‘Like’
    
- Attach a first message to your match requests 
    
- View new profiles in other cities
    
- Send vidéo messages to your matches 
    
- Feel safer with profile verification
    
- and much more.  
     
    

In addition to accessing all of these new ways to connect, your subscription will allow us to sustain ourselves as an app while continuing to offer free profiles to the neurodivergent people who need them.  
 

There are so few “by us, for us” resources for ND adults, so if you believe in our mission to smash the narrative of social isolation and shame surrounding the neurodivergent expérience – **if you want Hiki to continue to exist**– we need your support.  
 

We have big goals to offer even more to our community, from in-person events and conventions, to a safe social platform for neurodivergent teens, and more employment opportunities at Hiki for neurodivergent people. You can help us bring these dreams to life.  
 

Our team has worked hard to enhance your Hiki expérience, and we are honored to welcome all of the new faces and minds to this space.  
 

Thank you for being an integral part of who we are. We look forward to seeing the connections and magic you make in this community! We always welcome and encourage feedback— tap the Hiki logo in the app and send a support ticket, or email us at [help@hikiapp.com](mailto:help@hikiapp.com).
