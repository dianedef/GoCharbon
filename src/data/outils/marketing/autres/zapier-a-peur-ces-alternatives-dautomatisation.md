[**What are tasks?**](https://www.pabbly.com/connect/)

Any action being performed inside your workflow is considered a task. Triggers are not calculated in the tasks. Only the action steps are calculated in the task counts. Additionally, internal tasks of the Pabbly Connect such as Filter, Router, Formatter, etc are not counted as a task.

Like if you send one lead from Facebook Lead Ad to MailChimp, it will be counted as a single task. If you have a multi-step automation workflow, it will be counted depending upon the number of action steps. For example: If you send one lead from Facebook Lead Ad to MailChimp and then send the same lead to Google Sheets. It will be counted as 2 tasks. [Learn more](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YxK95UMwTD8)

[**What are Operations?**](https://www.pabbly.com/connect/)

Every check made to an external application to see if there is any new data in the entire day is counted towards the operation. So, if a check is made every 5 minutes. That is 288 operations in a day. Doesn’t matter if there is any new data or not. But in Pabbly Connect, we don’t charge anything for operations. That’s we say we’re providing Unlimited Operations.

For example: Let’s say you have a workflow to keep watching the Google My Business Page for replying to any new Google My Business review. Each check to Google My Business page to see if there is any new review posted is counted towards the operation. So doesn’t matter you send a reply or not, you will always spend 288 operations in a day
