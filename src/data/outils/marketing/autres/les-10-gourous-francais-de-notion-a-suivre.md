# Notion Ambassadors & Influencers

This page aims to list all the Notion Ambassadors & Influencers. Notion ambassadors are people passionated about Notion, who teach it and share content. It's very useful to follow them to learn how to use Notion in the most efficient way. You can find more details about what is a Notion Ambassador on [Notion's website](https://www.notion.so/99857c0d03e8431ab3c430d0afa1c1fe?pvs=21). Note that ambassadors are not necessarily Notion certified consultants.

This is not an exhaustive list. [You can contribute by suggesting an ambassador using the form at the end of this page](https://notionforms.io/notion-ambassadors-influencers#suggest-form).
