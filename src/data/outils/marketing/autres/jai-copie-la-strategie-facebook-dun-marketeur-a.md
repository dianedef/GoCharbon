# **This fucking works...**


That's just about 1 million dollars in sales so far this year thanks to this stupid simple process I'm going to share with you.

The great thing is, most of this process, once set up, is fully automated. So below you can see what I have been doing for about 3 years now to make money from my facebook friends list.

1. Add Friends Daily. I use friend connector software for this. If you don't have Friend Connector and aren't using it daily to grow your friends list you're basically throwing money in the toilet. You can get started with Friend Connector for FREE.
    
2. Create content on facebook. I go live and create posts pretty much daily (althogh I'm on a 30 day break from facebook right now learning how to build our own blockchain). I create content that provides value, allows my friends to get to know me, entertains them, and let's them know how I can help and what offers (things they can buy) I have. I use post scheduler software to create months of content in advanced and auto-post it for me so I don't have to do it manually.
    
3. Respond to comments on my posts. Many people don't do this for some reason, not sure why. But just responding to peoples comments on your posts go a long way. I use Post Profits to help me automate much of this process, but also manually do it sometimes.
    
4. Message fucking everyone. This is simple. Message everyone on your friends list and have conversations with them. I use Genius Messenger to send out broadcasts messages to everyone. I also use Birthday Wisher as well to message people before, on, and after their birthdays. This gives me more touchpoints with them and allows me to build a futher relationship with them which is key for sales. This is all also automated.
    
5. Respond to messages. Another easy one. REply to people who are messenging you and have conversations with them. I use MeFn EVan to reply automatically to messages I get asked a lot, this saves me a huge amount of time.
    
6. Make offers. Let people know what you have to offer them and how to buy it and some will buy.
    

And that's pretty much it. I've used this process to generate millions from facebook friends.
